Stay Safe on Freetown Roads

Important safety rules for Keke, Taxi & Poda Poda users

🚦 General Road Safety Tips

🚶 Pedestrians

Always use zebra crossings, especially at Cotton Tree, Congo Cross, and Lumley junctions.

📱 Avoid Distractions

Do not use your phone while crossing busy roads like Wilkinson Road or Kissy Road.

🌙 Night Travel

At night, prefer registered taxis instead of unknown Keke or bikes.

🛑 Road Awareness

Watch out for speeding vehicles around Hill Station and Circular Road.

🚖 Transport Safety

🛺 Keke Safety

Confirm fare before boarding. Avoid overloaded Keke at busy terminals like Lumley.

🚌 Poda Poda Safety

Do not enter overcrowded buses. Hold your belongings tightly.

🚕 Taxi Safety

Use trusted taxis. Share your trip location with family when possible.
